Fire and ice

My goddess,
I have waited so long for you.

Aeons have passed and still,
I sit alone,
in my kingdom of solitude.

With no one to share my world with.

Your voice is like the sweetest,
most harmonic melody,
caressing my ears.

Calmly,
softly,
soothing the fire in my soul.

I miss looking into your gorgeous,
magical eyes.

Love the way you play with your hair.

Adore your bright smile,
lighting up this cold and icy place.

So, I am asking.

What offerings do you want from me?

Where are you,
my love?

Fire and ice - Words long overdue
