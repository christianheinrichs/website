Translation update

This is a short text to let you know the current status of my website’s translation.

Back in spring 2019, which must have been at around April and May, I decided to translate my website into the Russian language. Since my knowledge of it is not that good, a native Russian speaker will notice obvious flaws and errors. The reason I decided to publish a translation anyway, is because the Russian language is part of who I am and I see no reason to hide the fact that I am of partially Slavic descent. I grew up in Germany and therefore consider the German language my native tongue and that’s also the reason you are seeing a German translation on this website.

All that being said however, my motivation in doing this kind of task is at the moment very low, so consider the Russian translation on hold, and the German translation being updated very infrequently, if at all.

Thank you.
